The IronDev's Wonder Trade Experiment

Intro

The mission behind this site is to clarify the generosity behind Pokemon X/Y's Wonder Trade System.

I ran a little experiment on 10/26/2013 to see what types of pokemon I got back.

So if you got an Eevee with a ____ that day, its very likely you were part of my experiment (thanks!)

The Experiment

So I broke this project into four parts...

  1. Breed a boat load of Eevees.Eevee is a pretty cool dude, so I bred 300 of him. I biked between Camphrier Town and Connecting Cave picking up eggs and from the day care as often as I could. I had a Talonflame in the front of my party (for the flame body bonus), and was constantly running the hatching o-power (with a level 4 recharge rate).
  2. Develop this sweet webapp.
  3. Record the data On Saturday 10/26/2013 I wonder traded 300 Eevees carrying Balm Mushrooms and recorded what I got back
  4. Analyze and disseminate the data Thats right, if you are interested in learning more, feel free to checkout the raw json file
